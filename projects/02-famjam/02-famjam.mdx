FamJam is an app that combined a real-time chat, shopping list, to-do list, and calendar. I developed the full-stack web application built on a MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node), with Firebase authentication, socket.io integration, and deployed through a CICD pipeline on Heroku.
